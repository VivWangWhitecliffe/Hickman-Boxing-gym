About Us

Welcome to Hickman Boxing Gym, where we offer competitive boxing classes that cater to individuals of all skill levels. As proud members of the Canterbury Boxing Association (CBA) and affiliated with the New Zealand Boxing Association, our commitment to the sport runs deep.

At Hickman Boxing Gym, we prioritize accessibility to the sport. As a non-profit organization, we strive to keep our rates affordable for everyone. Rest assured, every penny you invest in our classes goes directly back into our gym facilities and towards supporting the participation of our athletes in tournaments.

Join us in our journey to promote the art and discipline of boxing while fostering a strong sense of community within our walls. Experience the thrill of the sport with Hickman Boxing Gym.
